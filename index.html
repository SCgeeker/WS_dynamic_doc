Tips

  • Full TeX distribution installation before your first papaja article.
  • Manage “Title page” and “Abstract” in YAML.
  • Prepare your bibliography file and citation style while writing paper. Set them up in YAML(Guide by Frederik ).
  • Follow best practices
  • Check .tex and .log when you didn’t get what you want.
  • papaja is under development. Search the solutions and submit your questions at Github.
